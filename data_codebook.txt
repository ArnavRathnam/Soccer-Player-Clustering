Columns

Pos -- Position
  Position most commonly played by the player
  GK - Goalkeepers
  DF - Defenders
  MF - Midfielders
  FW - Forwards

90s -- 90s Played
  Minutes played divided by 90
  
G-PK -- Non-Penalty Goals
  Non-Penalty Goals
  I do not want penalty goals to impact the data
  
Ast -- Assists
  Assists
  
npxG -- npxG: Non-penalty Expected Goals
  Non-penalty Expected Goals
  npxG is the probabilty that a shot goes in based off of a variety of factors not including penalites
  
xAG -- xAG: Exp. Assisted Goals
  Expected Assisted Goals
  xG which follows a pass that assists a shot
  
PrgC -- Progressive Carries
  Carries that move the ball towards the opponent's goal line at least 10 yards from its furthest point in the last six   passes, or any carry into the penalty area. Excludes carries which end in the defending 50% of the pitch  
  
PrgP -- Progressive Passes
  Progressive Passes
  Completed passes that move the ball towards the opponent's goal line at least 10 yards from its furthest point in the   last six passes, or any completed pass into the penalty area. Excludes passes from the defending 40% of the pitch
  
Att -- Passes Attempted
  Passes Attempted
  
Cmp% -- Pass Completion %
  Pass Completion Percentage
  
Tkl -- Tackles
  Number of players tackled
  
Blocks -- Blocks
  Number of times blocking the ball by standing in its path

Int -- Interceptions
  Interceptions
  
Clr -- Clearances
  Clearances

TakeAtt -- Take-Ons Attempted
  Number of attempts to take on defenders while dribbling
  
Succ% -- Successful Take-On %
  Percentage of Take-Ons Completed Successfully
  Unsuccessful take-ons include attempts where the dribbler retained possession but was unable to get past the defender
